In this section, we turn our attention to describing, in some detail, several topics routinely associated with the study of artificial intelligence. These topics are:

  • Game playing and search

  • Automated reasoning

  • Neural networks and machine learning

Presentation of each topic is independent, so instructors can choose to cover any subset of the topics in any order. Interested students are encouraged to study on their own any of the topics that might be skipped by their instructor.
